The Horse Racing Predictor Project

As a dedicated team of three, we have developed a cutting-edge website that streams live and replay videos of horse races. Our platform not only provides real-time access to exhilarating horse racing events but also includes an innovative feature powered by a neural network model.
Users can upload a CSV file containing race data to get predictive insights on horse performance. Our neural network processes the data to predict the probability of different positions for the horse riders, helping users make informed betting decisions.
Join us as we revolutionize the horse racing experience through technology and data analytics!
